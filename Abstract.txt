Abstract: Online Movie ticket booking system 
This website is very useful considering the pandemic
Here on this website, I am coming up with an online movie ticket booking app
It contains two views one is for customer and another is for owner 
The customer can book the seats and also can cancel the tickets 
The moment he cancel or book the ticket he will be notified 
The owner can see how many seats have been filled and also can delete some seats if he wants.
login /register/logout will be there and based on the login the user will see their representative views
